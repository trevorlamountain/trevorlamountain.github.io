About Me

About Me

Currently based in Seattle, WA. I'm a physicist by training. Recent work has focused on hardware R&D in the AR/VR space. Professional interests include new media, human-machine interaction, decentralization, low-tech solutions, supply-chain sustainability, and new approaches to energy management and storage.

I'm also involved in a few winemaking and winegrowing projects in and around the peuget sound.

   

Projects

Projects that i've been working on

Trev's Wine

Farming small vineyards in unusual locations. Currently 0.7 acres of pinot noir and schoenburger on Whidbey Island and 0.3 acres of mixed vinifera and marquette in Ellensburg. This project aims to find honest expressions of this fruit by embracing the marginal climates they are grown in. It also connects me to grape-growing hobbiests outside of my typical social sphere.
